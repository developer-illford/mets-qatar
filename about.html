About Us

15+ Years

Your Trusted Partner in Testing Innovation

Middle East Testing Services (METS Lab) was established in 2018 by a group of technocrat researchers and leading test service professionals and educationists who have over 15 years of experience in the area of scientific research and development, administration, manufacturing and marketing, with an objective to provide excellence in testing services and to offer innovative and exceptional analytical process.

Why Choose METS Laboratories?

Mets Lab delivers reliable testing and analytical services, driven by quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Expertise And Experience

With years of dedicated service, our experts combine deep technical knowledge with a client-focused approach. From routine analysis to specialized testing, we deliver results that are scientifically sound and operationally impactful.

Innovative Solutions

Our team excels in creative thinking and practical problem-solving, going beyond standard testing to deliver tailored solutions for complex challenges. By embracing the latest technologies, we ensure clients get accurate results.

Quality and compliance

Quality is our foundation, not just a benchmark. We follow international standards with strict quality control to ensure every result meets the highest levels of accuracy, reliability, and compliance.
