Shelf Life Testing

How Long Will Your Product Last? Discover with Real-Time & Accelerated Shelf Life Testing

Shelf life testing is a critical process used to determine how long a product maintains its safety, quality, and functionality under specific storage conditions. At METS, we offer both Real-Time Shelf Life Testing and Accelerated Shelf Life Testing, tailored to meet the needs of a wide range of industries including food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and consumer goods. Both shelf life testing methods at METS are IAS-accredited and approved by the Ministry of Public Health (MOPH), ensuring compliance with international standards and regulatory requirements.



Real-Time Shelf Life Testing

What is Real-Time Shelf Life Testing?

Real-time shelf life testing involves storing a product under normal, recommended storage conditions (e.g., temperature, humidity, and light exposure) and monitoring it over time until it fails to meet quality specifications.

Key Features:

  • Conducted under actual storage conditions

  • Long duration (e.g., 6 months to 2 years or more)

  • Involves periodic testing (e.g., monthly, quarterly)

Advantages:

  • Provides the most accurate estimate of product shelf life

  • Required by regulators for certain product categories

Applications:

  • Final confirmation after accelerated testing

  • Stability testing for regulatory submissions

  • Long-term quality assurance






Accelerated Shelf Life Testing

What is Accelerated Shelf Life Testing?

Accelerated shelf life testing speeds up the aging process by storing products under elevated stress conditions (e.g., higher temperature, humidity, or light) to simulate long-term storage in a shorter period.

Key Features:

  • Uses predictive models such as the Arrhenius equation

  • Reduces testing time dramatically (from months to weeks)

  • Allows early estimation of product stability

Advantages:

  • Faster time-to-market for new products

  • Early identification of degradation issues

  • Cost-effective and time-efficient


Why Shelf Life Testing Is Important

Regulatory Compliance

Helps meet global standards such as FDA, ICH, ISO, GCC, SFDA, and other regional authorities.


Consumer Safety

Ensures the product remains safe for consumption or use throughout its shelf life.


Brand Integrity

Protects your brand reputation by maintaining consistent product quality.


Optimized Inventory Management

Accurate shelf life helps in better production, warehousing, and supply chain decisions.


Waste Reduction

Accurately predicted shelf life reduces early disposals and helps minimize product wastage.
