2. Regulatory Framework in Qatar & GCC

In the GCC, cosmetic and personal care products are regulated under harmonized Gulf standards to ensure safety, authenticity, and compliance.

Applicable Standards and References:

  • GSO 1943:2024 – Cosmetic Products – Safety Requirements of Cosmetics and Personal Care Products

  • GSO 2528:2024 – Technical Regulation of Cosmetic and Personal Care Product Claims

  • MoPH (Qatar Ministry of Public Health) – Import and market approval for cosmetics

  • QGOSM (Qatar General Organization for Standards & Metrology) – Conformity and pre-shipment verification


Key Regulatory Requirements:

  • All cosmetic products must comply with GSO 1943:2024 & GSO 2528:2024.

  • Test reports from ISO/IEC 17025-accredited laboratories are mandatory for conformity documentation.

  • Products must meet restricted and prohibited substance limits as per GSO/MoPH.

  • Non-compliant products may be rejected at customs or banned from sale.

3. Specific MoPH & QGOSM Testing Recommendations

QGOSM and MoPH have mandated several targeted chemical safety tests for cosmetics imported or manufactured in Qatar.

A. 1,4-Dioxane in Hair Care Products

  • Found as a byproduct in ingredients containing “xynol,” “eth,” “oleth,” “PEG,” or “ceteareth.”

  • Permissible Limit: ≤ 10 ppm (as per GSO 1943).

  • Mandatory for shampoos, conditioners, and hair treatments.

B. Formaldehyde in Keratin-Based Products

  • Applicable to hair straightening, keratin, or protein-based formulations.

  • Includes testing for formaldehyde releasers like DMDM Hydantoin, Imidazolidinyl Urea, Bronopol, and Quaternium-15.

  • Permissible Limit: As per GSO 1943:2024.

C. Phenylenediamine (PPD) in Hair Dye & Henna Products

  • Required for pre-shipment verification of henna and hair coloring formulations.

  • Ensures compliance with QGOSM’s restricted substance regulations.

5. Cosmetic Categories and Test Parameters

Below are typical test parameters and methods applied to various cosmetic categories tested at METS Lab:

A. Hair Care Products

Tests Include:

  • Active detergent matter content

  • pH, viscosity, moisture & volatile matter

  • Formaldehyde and 1,4-dioxane quantification

  • Parabens, ethanol, volatile organic compounds (VOCs)

  • Allergens, PPD, toxic chemicals

Applicable Methods & Standards:

ISO 2870:2009, ISO 2871-1:2010, ISO 2271:1989, ASTM D6173, ASTM D1681-05, GSO 1943, GSO 2161, GSO 2555, ISO 21150, ISO 18416, DIN 53019, SASO 2919, EAS 960

B. Body Care Products

Tests Include:

  • pH, total active matter, fatty matter, and alkali content
  • Antimicrobial activity and lather performance
  • Heavy metals (Pb, As, Hg) and toxic contaminants
  • Ethanol insoluble matter, phosphates, chlorides, allergens

Applicable Methods & Standards:

GSO 2019, GSO 1202, GSO 1894, ISO 2871-2, ISO 21149, ISO 22717, ISO 18416, SASO 2803, GSO 152/2007, GSO 2555, ISO 2870, EAS 847-20

C. Skin Care Products

Tests Include:

  • Hydroquinone, corticosteroids, and hydrogen peroxide
  • Parabens, phthalates, allergens, BHA, and PEGs
  • Synthetic colorants, mineral oils, and heavy metals
  • Microbiological purity and preservative efficacy

Applicable Standards:

SASO 2185, GSO 1152, GSO 205/1994, GSO 1046:2021, GSO 1943, GSO 1154/2002, GSO 1155/2002

D. Baby Care Products

Tests Include:

  • Heavy metal analysis and moisture content
  • pH, total fatty matter, and acid number
  • Lard or derivative detection
  • Parabens, phthalates, formaldehyde, talc, fragrance screening

Applicable Standards:

GSO 1895, GSO 1098, ISO 672, ISO 684

E. Oral Hygiene Products

Tests Include:

  • Fluoride concentration and pH
  • Heavy metals (Pb, Cd, As, Hg)
  • Abrasivity and stability against aging
  • Demineralization and strength determination

Applicable Standards:

ISO 16408:2004, ISO 11609:1995(E), ISO 28158:2018
